GAME RULES

A multiplayer game where all the participants thrive and compete against each other to reach first at the competition and become the ultimate winner. This championship will be played in multiple rounds and the winners of each rounds move on to the next until the final winner is selected.


The game rules are:

  1. All the players should join the game room at the fixed allotted date and time or else they will be considered as no show and eliminated
  2. The player should clear through all obstacles to reach the finish point
  3. There will be 5 laps in the game and the players need to complete all the laps
  4. If drones collide with each other or hit a fence or objects, the game will restart from the same place for that player alone
  5. Maximum of 5 collisions are allowed for one player after which the player will be eliminated
  6. The game will finish when the first 3 players cross the finish line
